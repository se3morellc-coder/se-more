Manual Tasks We Automate Away

Every business has its own flavor of busywork. Here are the most common workflows we tackle, and replace with systems that run themselves.

📄

Invoice & Payment Processing

Auto-match invoices to purchase orders, flag discrepancies the moment they appear, and trigger payment runs without a single click from your finance team.

👥

CRM Data Entry

Sync new contacts across platforms, auto-update records after every interaction, and log calls, emails, and meetings, all without your team lifting a finger.

📊

Report Generation

Schedule performance reports to build themselves and land in the right inboxes on time, every time. No more end-of-week scrambles to pull numbers together.

📧

Email & Notification Workflows

Trigger smart, contextual communications for customer onboarding, follow-up sequences, internal alerts, and escalations, all driven by real-time events, not manual sends.

📦

Inventory & Order Management

Maintain real-time sync across every sales channel, receive automatic low-stock alerts, and trigger reorder workflows before you ever run out of what customers need.

🧑‍💼

Employee Onboarding

Automatically provision accounts, route documents for signature, kick off training checklists, and notify managers at each milestone, so new hires hit the ground running.
