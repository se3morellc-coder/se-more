Technology Audit

Deep review of your current stack, costs, and capability gaps. Understand exactly what you have, what it's costing you, and where the cracks are before making your next move.

Build vs Buy Analysis

Objective assessment of whether to build custom software or adopt existing tools. We weigh long-term cost, control, speed, and risk so you choose the path that actually makes sense.

Vendor Selection

Evaluate and recommend the right software vendors for your needs. We cut through sales pitches and feature bloat to match you with tools that fit your team, budget, and goals.
